The Embrace Foundation

Unleash the Kingdom

Here at the Embrace Foundation are mission is to restore broken souls to wholeness through Spiritual Healing, Encouragement, Community Outreach and Love.

Whether its through engaging the community, group training or one on one personal development The Embrace Foundation is commited to love the unlovable with Gods devine truth about who we are and who we are truly meant to be.

Welcome to The Embrace Foundation where we adhere to the motto I am on Purpose because we absolutely believe that along with all creation You Are on Purpose. Are goal is to reinforce the individual as well as family spirituality in an attempt to strengthen our communities through family events and interaction. as a client of the Embrace Foundation, you can rest assured that your physical, mental and spiritual wellbeing will always be the focused outcome of are programs.
